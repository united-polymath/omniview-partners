Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

OmniView Partners ("OmniView," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website, assessments, platform, and coaching services (together, the "Services"), you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Assessment responses and the multi-dimensional results derived from them.
  • Coaching session notes, goals, and messages you share with your coach.
  • Inquiry and partnership details you submit through our forms.

Information collected automatically

  • Device, browser, and usage data, including pages viewed and interactions.
  • Cookies and similar technologies used to operate and improve the Services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver the Services — generating your profile report and matching you with a vetted coach.
  • To personalize coaching, including AI-assisted prompts and progress tracking.
  • To communicate with you about your account, sessions, and requests.
  • To improve our methods, instruments, and platform, including research conducted by OmniView Labs in de-identified, aggregate form.
  • To maintain security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as necessary:

  • With your dedicated coach, to deliver your coaching.
  • With service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • For organizational clients, only aggregate, privacy-respecting insights are shared with people leaders — never individual responses without consent.
  • When required by law, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services, and thereafter as required to meet legal, accounting, or reporting obligations. You may request deletion as described below.

5. Security

We apply administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your data and continuously improve our practices.

6. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at omniview12@gmail.com.

7. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep the site functioning, remember preferences, and understand usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect some features.

8. International Transfers

We are based in the Republic of Korea. Where information is processed outside your country, we take steps to ensure appropriate protection consistent with this Policy.

9. Children's Privacy

The Services are intended for adults and are not directed to children under 14. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version here and update the "Last updated" date. Material changes may be communicated directly.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Policy or your data, contact us at omniview12@gmail.com, OmniView Partners, Seoul, Republic of Korea.

This Privacy Policy is provided as general information and does not constitute legal advice.
